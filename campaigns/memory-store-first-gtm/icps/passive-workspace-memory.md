# Passive Workspace Memory

Status: proposed unconventional ICP
Target first batch: 10 accounts after shadow sample
Primary learning intent: unconventional persona and pain-language fit

## Persona

Product ops lead, founder/operator, chief of staff, support/community lead, CS/FDE operator, or meeting-heavy team owner.

## Pain

The important answer is buried in standup, Slack, meeting notes, customer messages, or a prior agent thread, so teammates and agents keep asking again.

## Hard Criteria

- Meeting-heavy or remote-work signal.
- Product, customer, support, community, or operations workflow is visible.
- Public tool/workflow signal exists.
- Operator buyer can be identified.

## Trigger Signals

- Visible use of Slack, Fathom, Fireflies, Granola, Linear, Notion, customer communities, or meeting-heavy workflows.
- Public posts about internal coordination, customer context, standups, product priorities, or AI agents.
- Remote/distributed hiring or cross-functional operating load.

## Proof Path

Show a living status/context readout over meetings and Slack, then ask whether the team's agents should read the same context.

## Exclusions

- Teams without visible cross-tool operating context.
- Accounts where meeting-heavy status is inferred but not sourceable.
- Any row where the person does not own coordination, customer, product, or support context.

## Websets Query

Remote or meeting-heavy product, support, community, CS, or founder-led teams using Slack, Fathom, Fireflies, Granola, Linear, or Notion where team and agent context is fragmented across meetings and messages.
