# GTM Context Operators

Status: proposed
Target first batch: 20 accounts after shadow sample
Primary learning intent: signal quality and persona fit

## Persona

Founder-led GTM owner, GTM engineer, sales engineer, outbound agency operator, or technical growth lead.

## Pain

ICP theory, account context, objections, proof paths, and reply learning do not survive across campaign batches, tools, and agents.

## Hard Criteria

- B2B company, technical services firm, outbound agency, or AI/GTM tooling team.
- Visible GTM, outbound, RevOps, sales-engineering, or founder-led sales motion.
- A sourceable current signal stronger than company existence.
- Buyer/persona can be identified.

## Trigger Signals

- Hiring or posting about GTM engineering, AI SDRs, signal-based outbound, RevOps automation, or sales engineering.
- Public use of Clay, Apollo, Smartlead, Instantly, or account-brief workflows.
- Founder/operator posts about outbound quality, ICP refinement, reply learning, or customer/account context.

## Proof Path

Show the GTM plan, signal-card quality gate, and how approved replies and objections become Memory Store learning for the next batch.

## Exclusions

- Pure bulk-email operators without evidence quality.
- Lead-gen agencies that only want volume.
- Current customers unless expansion is explicitly approved.

## Websets Query

Founder-led B2B teams, AI GTM teams, GTM engineering teams, and outbound agencies using signal-based outbound, Clay, Apollo, Smartlead, AI SDRs, or account brief workflows where ICP theory and reply learning must persist across batches.
