# Customer Delivery Memory Teams

Status: proposed
Target first batch: 15 accounts after shadow sample
Primary learning intent: proof-path resonance

## Persona

CS lead, FDE lead, onboarding lead, implementation lead, customer ops founder, or solutions lead.

## Pain

Customer promises, onboarding state, implementation context, support language, and next actions live across calls, Slack, docs, tickets, and email.

## Hard Criteria

- Customer-delivery workflow is visible.
- Team sells or implements a non-trivial product or service.
- Recent hiring, case study, docs, implementation, onboarding, or customer proof signal.
- CS/FDE/onboarding buyer can be identified.

## Trigger Signals

- Hiring for solutions, FDE, implementation, onboarding, customer success, or customer ops.
- Public implementation-heavy case studies.
- Docs/changelog/customer rollout evidence.
- Visible meeting-heavy or customer-workflow surface.

## Proof Path

Show a small account/customer brief or living playbook generated from real context.

## Exclusions

- Low-complexity self-serve products with no onboarding or customer-success motion.
- Teams where the buyer cannot be identified.
- Accounts with only generic homepage/category fit.

## Websets Query

Customer-success, FDE, onboarding, solutions, implementation, and customer ops teams at knowledge-heavy B2B companies where customer context lives across calls, Slack, docs, tickets, and email.
