# Product Customer Insight Teams

Status: proposed expanded ICP
Target first batch: 15 accounts after shadow sample
Primary learning intent: customer-insight proof-path resonance

## Persona

Product lead, founder-PM, customer researcher, product ops lead, support/product hybrid, or product marketing lead.

## Pain

Feedback, objections, docs gaps, feature asks, customer language, and promises scatter across calls, Slack, tickets, docs, support, and agent threads.

## Hard Criteria

- Product or customer-insight workflow is visible.
- Team has repeated customer touchpoints.
- Recent public signal indicates feedback, roadmap, support, docs, or customer-learning pressure.

## Trigger Signals

- Hiring product ops, user research, customer insights, support ops, or product marketing.
- Public feedback programs, changelog velocity, docs churn, customer interviews, support themes, or roadmap posts.
- Visible customer-facing Slack/community, support, review, or interview-heavy workflow.

## Proof Path

Show a compact customer-insight brief: repeated language, objections, promises, docs gaps, feature asks, and recommended next actions.

## Exclusions

- Teams with no public customer touchpoint evidence.
- Pure analytics teams where qualitative customer context is not visible.
- Accounts where the signal is only generic product-led growth language.

## Websets Query

B2B product, product ops, customer research, support-product, or product marketing teams with visible customer feedback, docs churn, changelog velocity, support themes, or roadmap activity where customer language and decisions need to persist across tools.
