# Internal AI Platform Teams

Status: proposed expanded ICP
Target first batch: 10 accounts for validation
Primary learning intent: internal-agent platform readiness

## Persona

Internal AI lead, platform engineer, automation lead, AI transformation owner, RevOps automation lead, IT automation lead.

## Pain

Internal agents need approved rules, constraints, handoffs, customer or company context, and decision history before they can act across workflows.

## Hard Criteria

- Company has a visible internal AI, automation, platform, or agent workflow initiative.
- Buyer/owner can be identified.
- Signal shows agents or automation crossing tools or teams.

## Trigger Signals

- Hiring internal AI, AI automation, AI enablement, platform, agent workflow, or automation roles.
- Public posts about rolling out agents internally, AI governance, workflow automation, copilots, or productivity agents.
- Visible multi-tool operating stack where agents need approvals, policies, constraints, and shared context.

## Proof Path

Show an internal agent context profile: what the agent is allowed to know, approved rules, open loops, handoff state, and action constraints.

## Exclusions

- Enterprise accounts with no accessible buyer path.
- Generic AI adoption statements without workflow evidence.
- Teams where procurement/security cycle is clearly too heavy for this first campaign.

## Websets Query

Companies hiring internal AI, AI automation, platform, AI enablement, RevOps automation, or agent workflow roles where internal agents need approved company context, rules, handoffs, and decisions across tools.
