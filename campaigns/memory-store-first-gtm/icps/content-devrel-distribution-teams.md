# Content Devrel Distribution Teams

Status: proposed expanded ICP
Target first batch: 10 accounts after shadow sample
Primary learning intent: distribution memory and voice fit

## Persona

Founder-led distribution owner, devrel lead, content operator, community-led growth lead, product marketer.

## Pain

The best post, launch angle, customer story, objection, or market language is usually buried in customer calls, Slack, docs, demos, and prior agent work.

## Hard Criteria

- Public content, devrel, launch, founder-led writing, or community-led growth motion.
- The team has repeated source material from calls, customers, community, docs, or launches.
- Buyer or sender can be identified.

## Trigger Signals

- Frequent launches, founder posts, technical blogs, community content, podcasts, webinars, or product storytelling.
- Public customer calls, case studies, devrel/community activity, or launch campaigns.
- Hiring content, devrel, community, product marketing, or founder-office roles.

## Proof Path

Show a content angle or launch brief recovered from customer calls, Slack, docs, and prior work, with voice constraints preserved.

## Exclusions

- Content teams with no product/customer source material.
- Pure SEO content mills.
- Teams where the primary pain is distribution volume rather than context/voice/source reuse.

## Websets Query

Founder-led distribution teams, devrel teams, content operators, community-led growth teams, or product marketers with frequent launches, technical blogs, customer stories, webinars, podcasts, or community activity where market language and voice need to persist across tools.
