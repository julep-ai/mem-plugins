# Agencies Consultancies Multi Client Memory

Status: proposed expanded ICP
Target first batch: 15 accounts after shadow sample
Primary learning intent: multi-client delivery memory fit

## Persona

AI agency founder, GTM agency operator, implementation consultant, product studio lead, RevOps consultant, solutions consultant.

## Pain

Every client has separate context, promises, workflows, objections, approvals, artifacts, and next actions. Agents need the right client context without mixing projects.

## Hard Criteria

- Multi-client service or consulting model is visible.
- The team sells AI, GTM, implementation, product, RevOps, or customer-delivery services.
- Public case studies, client roster, service pages, or client-workflow evidence exist.

## Trigger Signals

- Publishing case studies, client stories, playbooks, or implementation offers.
- Hiring delivery, GTM, automation, solutions, or client success roles.
- Posts about client context, handoff, onboarding, delivery quality, or AI agents.

## Proof Path

Show a client-memory workspace or account brief that keeps each client context separate and retrievable.

## Exclusions

- Commodity agencies with no complex client context.
- Firms that only want lead lists or template generation.
- Accounts where client context cannot be safely referenced or separated.

## Websets Query

AI agencies, GTM agencies, implementation consultancies, product studios, RevOps consultancies, or solutions consulting teams serving multiple clients where client-specific context, promises, deliverables, approvals, and next actions need to persist across agents.
