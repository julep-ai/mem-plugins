# Research Knowledge Workers

Status: exploratory expanded ICP
Target first batch: 10 accounts or people after shadow sample
Primary learning intent: high-context individual and team fit

## Persona

AI researcher, analyst, consultant, strategy lead, legal/finance operator, or high-context independent expert.

## Pain

Sources, reasoning chains, decisions, taste constraints, open questions, and client/project context disappear across sessions, notebooks, documents, meetings, and agents.

## Hard Criteria

- High-context, long-running, source-heavy work is visible.
- Person or team uses AI, agents, notebooks, research workflows, or knowledge tools.
- Public signal is specific enough to avoid generic knowledge-worker targeting.

## Trigger Signals

- Long-form research, reports, papers, notebooks, investigations, repeated client work, or source-heavy workflows.
- Public writing about AI research, complex projects, strategy, legal/financial analysis, or knowledge systems.
- Visible tool use around Obsidian, Notion, research agents, coding notebooks, or source management.

## Proof Path

Show a research/context brief that preserves source trails, unresolved questions, decisions, and what remains binding for the next session.

## Exclusions

- Generic knowledge workers with no visible long-running context problem.
- One-off researchers without repeated workflow.
- Prospects where privacy/scope concerns overwhelm the first-touch proof path.

## Websets Query

AI researchers, analysts, consultants, strategy teams, legal or finance operators, and high-context independent experts using AI, notebooks, source-heavy workflows, or knowledge systems where reasoning chains, sources, decisions, and open questions must persist across sessions.
