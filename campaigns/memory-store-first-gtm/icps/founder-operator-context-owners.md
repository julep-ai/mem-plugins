# Founder Operator Context Owners

Status: proposed expanded ICP
Target first batch: 15 accounts after shadow sample
Primary learning intent: founder/operator pain fit

## Persona

Founder, chief of staff, early operator, founder-PM, or technical operator at an AI or B2B startup.

## Pain

Investor asks, customer promises, product priorities, hiring decisions, and agent work all fragment across meetings, Slack, email, docs, and AI tools.

## Hard Criteria

- Early-stage AI or B2B company with visible operating complexity.
- Founder/operator owns multiple workstreams.
- Public signal shows context fragmentation, agent usage, rapid growth, or cross-functional coordination load.

## Trigger Signals

- YC, accelerator, launch, funding, hiring, or rapid team-growth signal.
- Founder posts about agents, internal ops, customer promises, investor asks, hiring, or losing context across tools.
- Visible use of Slack, Notion, Linear, Gmail, meetings, Claude, Codex, Cursor, or multiple operating surfaces.

## Proof Path

Show a founder operating-memory readout: what is still binding, open loops, customer promises, recent decisions, and what the next agent needs before acting.

## Exclusions

- Founders with no visible AI/agent or context-heavy workflow.
- Teams where the only signal is funding.
- Companies too large for founder-led adoption unless an operator buyer is visible.

## Websets Query

Early-stage AI and B2B founders or operators using agents, Slack, meetings, Linear, Notion, Claude, Codex, or Cursor where customer promises, investor context, hiring, and product priorities are fragmented across tools.
